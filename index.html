Details

I usually start feeding the animals in the am at like 5:00 - 6:00 AM... so if you are staying here, they will start trying to wak you up around that time. Chicken + Rice = microwave 1 cup (dry) instant rice + about 1.5 cup water + 2 tbsp butter for 5 min. When cooked add 2-3 handfulls of frozen chicken and mix together. Let cool about 2-3 min. When you feed the dogs, lock Pajama in his crate and close the laundry room (pocket door) behind you. Bella will eventually get out, but it will make it easier for you to get into the yard without being bothered. Also close the "Zombie cage" behind you. The goats can fit in between the bars, but the dogs can't, so it will allow the goats to escape to a safe place if needed. The dogs water bowl in the kitchen needs to be refilled 1-2 times per day. Let the dogs out into the yard for about 10 minutes after you have finished feeding the goats. NOTE- if you stay in the yard with them the entire time, they probably will not poop outside, but will later shit in the hallway in the house. So for at least a few minutes they need to be alone out there. If you would like to play with Luke, he needs to be in the house alone with you. Bella gets very confrontational and jealous if you pay too much attention to Luke when she is around.
