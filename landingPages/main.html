Details

I usually start feeding the animals in the am at like 5:00 - 7:00 AM... so if you are staying here, they will start trying to wake you up around that time...sorry :/ Jams is picky and may not eat all (most) food Toss whatever he doesn't want to eat. Chicken and Veg A pot of chicken and veg

When you arrive / or are ready to feed the dogs:

  1. Let Xena and Huey out back
  2. Close the dogs outside the rot-iron "zombie cage" (around the back patio)
  3. Lock the back door and leave the two dogs out for a few minutes, so they will go potty instead of trying to get you to play with them
  4. Let pajama out of our bedroom and tell him to go into his crate
  5. Follow him into laundry room and lock him into his crate -- give a tug to the cage to confirm that it is securely locked
  6. Open the zombie gate and back door to let the two back in -- leave the zombie gate and back door open while you get jams
  7. Tell Huey to go into his crate
  8. Lock Huey in his crate tug twice to confirm its secure
  9. While standing in front of Huey's crate, unlock 'Jams crate and tell him to go outside
  10. Follow pajama to the back door, get him closed out of the zombie gate and lock him out for a few minutes
  11. Put chicken and veg (and doggy dailies if AM feeding) from fridge into each dogs' bowl
  12. Let Pajama back in and tell him to go into his crate / Lock him into his crate
  13. Their typical feeding order is: 1 Xena (all metal bowl), 2 Huey (blue bottom metal bowl) 3 Pajama (blue plastic bowl),
  14. After they have finished eating, put Huey and Xena outside --outside of the zombie gate and let them go potty (while Pajama waits in his crate)
  15. Let the two back in and leave the zombie gate and back door open
  16. Put Huey back into his crate, check the lock, then, while standing in front of Huey's crate let Jams out and tell him to "go outside"
  17. follow Jams out and lock him outside (while Huey is still locked in his crate)
  18. After Jams has had a few minutes and/or hopefully pooped, let him back in and say "go to the bedroom" follow him to our bedroom and lock him in
  19. Let Huey out
  20. Make sure all doors are locked before you leave
The dogs water bowl in the entry hall needs to be refilled 1-2 times per day. Pajama usually drinks out of the master bedroom toilet.

The Chickens

Overview

AM PM
1.) (after feeding dogs and settling them inside) get the dish of treats for the chickens from the fridge and put on a pair of gloves (2) go out to the "barn" and grab the black plastic "mixing bowl" from the central structure, dump the fridge treats in the mixing bowl and go into the she-shed and get 1 (choabi) scoop of chicken feed pellets (whatever bag is open) and add them to the mixing bow. toss everything together in the mixing bowl (I usually just use the bowl to toss it and mix everything, but you can mix with your gloved hand too.) (3) throw handfuls of feed to various areas of the chicken coop, so the chickens can spread out to eat. I usually let them roam in the goat corral all day (there is one chicken that occasionally tries to go in the main yard... if she does that just keep the dogs inside and try to shoo her back into the goat area, if she goes in the neighbors yard she will eventually come back, but don't let her stay in our main yard if you see her there) Feeding is the same as the AM, but if you are comfortable, please sidle past the cinder blocks and check the nests for eggs. ALSO - I usually coop all the chickens up around 4-6PM and keep them closed in until the morning (there are 9 chickens total 4 beige, 4 white and 1 black)
