  12. Let Pajama back in and tell him to go into his crate / Lock him into his crate
  13. Their typical feeding order is: 1 Pajama (blue plastic bowl), 2 Xena (all metal bowl), 3 Huey (blue bottom metal bowl)

  17. follow Jams out and lock him outside (while Huey is still locked in his crate)
  18. After Jams has had a few minutes and/or hopefully pooped, let him back in and say "go to the bedroom" follow him to our bedroom and lock him in

  20. Let Huey out
  21. Make sure all doors are locked, the dry wall panel closed and lights are all out before you leave

Measuring and adding nutrients

I'll leave 3 "mixing containers" (old nutrient bottles), a tablespoon a funnel and the required nutrients on the kitchen counter next to the fridge.
All of the plants recieve the same nutrient mix: 1 tablespoon of "calMag", 1 tablespoon of "bloom nutirent", and 1 tablespoon of "grow nutrient"... use the funnel to put the nutrients into the bottles --then fill the bottle up with water after the nutrients are added.
To add nutrients to the plants, lift up the lid of each plant bucket and pour in nutrient water enough to bring the level into the bucket up to just below the bottom of the "root basket".
NOTE - You may not need all three gallons each day
